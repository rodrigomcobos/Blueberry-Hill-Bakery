About Us

Baking with Love

Since 1994

We come to Freeport after years as the original owners of LaLoba’s Bakery in Santa Rosa Beach. Our goal has always been to produce fresh, natural, organic foods and to educate folks about how delicious truly fresh and organic products are when properly prepared. In recent years we have become acutely aware of the growing need for wholesome, fresh foods which take into account the restrictive dietary requirements such as gluten free, grain free, nut free, soy free, and vegan.
