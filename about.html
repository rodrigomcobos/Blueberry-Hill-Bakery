In recent years we have become acutely aware of the growing need for wholesome, fresh foods which take into account the restrictive dietary requirements such as gluten free, grain free, nut free, soy free, and vegan.

Our Delicious Recipes
Made With Love

Blueberry Hill Bakery is Freeport’s new, friendly neighborhood bakery/cafe specializing in fresh, made from scratch baked goods and healthy lunch fare!

We utilize as much organic and locally produced ingredients as possible.We offer a wide selection of lunch and bakery items which are gluten free and vegan. We are not certified gluten free as our kitchen also produces baked goods containing wheat and gluten but we use great care to avoid cross contamination. Blueberry Hill is a small family eatery with outdoor picnic tables so our menu is primarily to go items.





All Natural
Baked Goods

We have developed many recipes to accommodate these special needs and are continuing to expand our offerings. We welcome suggestions, questions, and special requests.

All of our products are free of soy and artificial colors, flavors, and preservatives. If you have a very serious allergy, please recognize that our kitchen does process wheat, dairy, eggs, and nuts. Please stop by and say hello when you are in the neighborhood and sample some of our fresh and tasty offerings.
